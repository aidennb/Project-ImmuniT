## Description

ImmuniT transforms a single blood test into a complete map of your immune system.

Powered by PepSeq peptide microarray technology, ImmuniT analyzes your antibody reactivity across 100,000+ peptide sequences to deliver personalized insights about your vaccine protection, autoimmune risk, allergen sensitivity, and neuroprotective health — all in one app.

VACCINE TRACKER
Track every vaccination with real-time antibody levels, not just dates. See your actual protection status for COVID-19, Influenza, Tetanus, Hepatitis, MMR, and more. Get personalized booster recommendations based on your declining antibody titers — not generic schedules.

AUTOIMMUNITY RADAR
Monitor autoimmune markers like TPO, ANA, RF, and GAD65. Visualize your risk profile on an interactive radar chart with population comparisons. Get flagged when markers exceed clinical thresholds.

ALLERGEN EXPLORER
Map your reactivity to food and environmental allergens. Track seasonal patterns, log symptoms in the built-in journal, and share your allergen profile with healthcare providers.

COGNITIVE PROTECTION
Track neuroprotective antibodies against beta-amyloid, tau, TDP-43, and Poly-GA — biomarkers associated with Alzheimer's, ALS, and other neurodegenerative conditions. Early detection that traditional screening misses.

IMMUNITY PASSPORT
Generate a verifiable health passport with your vaccination records, allergen profile, and immunity summary. Share securely with doctors, travel authorities, or clinical trial coordinators.

AI HEALTH ASSISTANT
Ask questions about your immunity data in plain English. Get personalized answers based on your actual test results, not generic information.

BUILT FOR PRIVACY
- HIPAA-compliant architecture
- AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit
- AWS Cognito authentication
- Your data is never sold to third parties
- Full data deletion on request (GDPR/CCPA)

ImmuniT was developed in collaboration with immunology researchers at Arizona State University using PepSeq peptide microarray technology.
