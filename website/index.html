Powered by PepSeq

From 100,000 peptides to actionable insights.

Our platform transforms raw PepSeq microarray data into clinically meaningful immune intelligence.

01

Sample Collection

A single blood draw is processed through PepSeq peptide microarray technology, testing reactivity against 100,000+ peptide sequences.

02

Z-Score Analysis

Raw reactivity data is normalized into Z-scores against population baselines, flagging statistically significant elevations like Poly-GA at 41.95σ.

03

Risk Classification

Our algorithm classifies markers across vaccine protection, autoimmune risk, allergen reactivity, and neuroprotective status into actionable categories.

04

Personalized Dashboard

Results appear in your mobile app within minutes — with trend tracking, booster recommendations, and critical alerts for specialist referrals.

About ImmuniT

Built at the intersection of immunology and technology.

ImmuniT was born from clinical research at ASU, where PepSeq peptide microarray technology revealed that comprehensive immune profiling could detect health risks months before traditional screening.

Our platform transforms complex peptide reactivity data into clear, actionable insights — from vaccine booster timing to early neurodegenerative biomarker detection.
