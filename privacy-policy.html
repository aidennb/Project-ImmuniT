Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

ImmuniT ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal and health information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password (managed by AWS Cognito).
  • Health Data: Vaccination records, immunity test results (PepSeq peptide microarray data), autoimmune markers, allergen reactivity, and neuroprotective antibody levels — entered by you or your healthcare provider.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting and performance optimization.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display your personalized immunity dashboard and health insights.
  • Generate vaccine booster recommendations based on your antibody levels.
  • Calculate risk scores for autoimmune and neurodegenerative markers.
  • Produce immunity passports for travel and healthcare provider sharing.
  • Improve app functionality and fix bugs.

We do not sell your personal or health data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in AWS infrastructure (DynamoDB, S3) in the us-west-2 region.
  • Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+).
  • Authentication is managed by AWS Cognito with industry-standard JWT tokens.
  • API access requires authentication and API key verification.
  • We follow the principle of least privilege for all system access.

4. HIPAA Compliance

ImmuniT is designed to comply with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect Protected Health Information (PHI). Our AWS infrastructure is configured in accordance with the AWS HIPAA Eligible Services framework.

5. Data Sharing

We share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • At your direction: When you use the "Send to Doctor" or sharing features.
  • Service providers: AWS provides hosting infrastructure under a Business Associate Agreement.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • Research (opt-in only): De-identified aggregate data may be used for immunology research, only with your explicit consent.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your health data within the app at any time.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information through the app.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us. We will comply within 30 days (GDPR/CCPA).
  • Export: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, all personal and health data is permanently removed within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for research purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

ImmuniT is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Cloud hosting, authentication (Cognito), database (DynamoDB), storage (S3).
  • Expo / EAS: App build and distribution infrastructure.

We do not integrate third-party advertising or analytics SDKs that track users across apps.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
